Objectives of this Project:
1. Build a text summariser taking News articles and building the summary and identifying the sentiment.
2. It will have 4 sub-projects:
    1. Web scrapper - It will scrap daily news about NIFTY 100 companies from Google and will store the articles text and heading.
    2. Text summariser - It will build a summary of the articles and provide a view about different companies. This will be implemented
                         using 2 different approaches Extractive summarization and Abstractive summarization.
    3. Sentiment Analysis - It will identify the sentiment about the companies in market for last 20 days. The sentiment will be
                         categorized in Positive, Negative and Neutral. This will be enhanced later.
